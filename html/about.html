Strong Commitment, Strong Roots About Our Nursery

Founded in 2021 by Benjamin and Whitney Clements in the first year of their marriage, the Flower Factory truly is a Family Buiness! Benjamin grew up working at his parents nursery in Idaho and developed a passion for horticulture and business at a young age. The couple decided to take the best of their knowlege and expereince and open a nursery focused on providing exceptional annuals, made afoordable by growing them here oursleves! Our priorities here at Flower Factory are to wow customers with product diversity and availability and offer these products at wonderful prices!

We guarantee that you will:

  • Be greeted and helped by friendly staff
  • see the value of locally-grown products
  • and leave feeling like you got a good deal!
